I’m an east-coast girl with a suitcase heart. Among other things, I'm a big dreamer, a from-the-gut howling laugher, a pop culture and media studies junky, a twenty-something still hopeful there’s time to change the things that matter in the world.

San Francisco has been home for several years, and I work at a fast-paced, scrappy startup that wants to make a difference in the way women shop. When I'm not working I'm updating my Goodreads with the latest book, whipping up jams, pickles and other concoctions in the kitchen, and exploring as much as possible to itch that travel bug.
